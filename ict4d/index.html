For the past years the ICT4D community has notably grown and continues to do so as every month over a thousand links are shared through the hashtags #ICT4D, #M4D and others. Although the term ICT4D has been extensively argued for a number of reasons, it still works as a cluster to find out more on potentials and challenges for ICTs in the developemt context.

It is not easy, but also time consuming, to find the best resources related to the topic. Therefore, I have started mining such tweets and resources to facilitate and provide them to the ICT4D community. My @ict4d acccount was always meant to be a twitter account for people in search of great resources around ICT4D. Now, this is a weekly service to deliver a list of the best trending resources to your inbox. Enjoy and send me feedback any time @ict4d or comment below.
