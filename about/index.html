This blog aims to analyze potentials in the interface of technology and social innovation, and also the challenges of the digital age.

The blog is written by Christian Kreutz:
  • The director of crisscrossed GmbH, the firm behind the innovative idea sharing tool called WE THINQ.
  • The co-founder and board member of the German Open Knowledge Foundation.
  • Lecturer at University of Darmstadt 2011-2012
  • Founder of Create Frankfurt (frankfurt-gestalten.de), a ground breaking citizen participation network for local politics, which has received Germany-wide media attention.
  • He has worked with various international and national organizatons such as the World Bank, KfW developing bank, GIZ, UNDP and many others.
